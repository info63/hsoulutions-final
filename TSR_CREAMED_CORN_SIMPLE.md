## INGREDIENTS

**CORN & DAIRY (61.8% - The Base):**
- 3 cups frozen corn niblets (thawed)
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 4 oz cream cheese (softened)

**CHEESE & FLAVOR (38.2% - The Enhancement):**
- 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese (shredded)
- 1/4 cup butter (4 tablespoons)
- 1 sleeve Ritz crackers (crushed)
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

**OPTIONAL EXTRAS:**
- 2 tablespoons fresh chives (chopped)
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon bacon bits

---

## DIRECTIONS

**PREP:**
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
2. Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish
3. Thaw corn completely, drain well

**MAKE SAUCE:**
1. In large saucepan over medium heat:
   - Melt 1/4 cup butter
   - Add cream cheese, stir until smooth (2-3 min)
   - Add heavy cream and sour cream
   - Whisk until combined
   - Heat until just bubbling (don't boil)

2. Remove from heat, stir in:
   - Sugar, salt, pepper, garlic powder
   - Taste and adjust

**ASSEMBLE:**
1. In large bowl, combine:
   - Thawed corn
   - Hot cream mixture
   - 3/4 cup cheddar cheese (save 1/4 cup)
   - Chives and Parmesan (if using)

2. Pour into baking dish

**TOP:**
1. Mix together:
   - Crushed Ritz crackers
   - 2 tablespoons melted butter
   - Remaining 1/4 cup cheddar
   - Bacon bits (if using)

2. Sprinkle evenly over corn

**BAKE:**
- Bake at 350Â°F for 25-30 minutes
- Until bubbly and golden brown
- Let rest 5 minutes before serving

---

## QUICK TIPS

**Too thick?** Add 1/4 cup more cream
**Too thin?** Add more crackers on top
**Want it richer?** Add 2 more tablespoons butter
**Want it lighter?** Use half-and-half instead of heavy cream
