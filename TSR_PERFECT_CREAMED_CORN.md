## INGREDIENTS (TSR-BALANCED)

### BASE LAYER (61.8% - Foundation)
- **3 cups** frozen corn niblets (thawed)
- **1 cup** heavy cream OR half-and-half
- **1/2 cup** sour cream
- **4 oz** cream cheese (softened)

### ENHANCEMENT LAYER (38.2% - Flavor)
- **1 cup** shredded sharp cheddar cheese
- **1/4 cup** butter (4 tablespoons)
- **1 sleeve** Ritz crackers (crushed, about 1 cup)
- **2 tablespoons** sugar (balances corn sweetness)
- **1 teaspoon** salt
- **1/2 teaspoon** black pepper
- **1/4 teaspoon** garlic powder (optional but recommended)
- **1/4 teaspoon** paprika (adds depth)

### TSR ADDITIONS (OGH Recommendations)
- **2 tablespoons** fresh chives OR green onions (chopped)
- **1/4 cup** grated Parmesan cheese (umami boost)
- **Pinch** cayenne pepper (for those who want heat)
- **1 tablespoon** bacon bits (optional - adds smokiness)

## INSTRUCTIONS (Ï†-OPTIMIZED METHOD)

### STEP 1: PREPARE (Foundation)
1. Preheat oven to **350Â°F** (Ï†-adjusted temperature - not too hot, not too cool)
2. Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish
3. Thaw frozen corn completely, drain well

### STEP 2: CREATE BASE (The 61.8%)
1. In large saucepan over **medium heat**:
   - Melt butter
   - Add cream cheese, stir until smooth (2-3 minutes)
   - Add heavy cream, sour cream - whisk until combined
   - Heat until just bubbling (DO NOT BOIL)

2. Remove from heat, add:
   - Sugar, salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika
   - Stir until sugar dissolves
   - Taste and adjust seasoning

### STEP 3: COMBINE (The Unity)
1. In large mixing bowl:
   - Add thawed corn
   - Pour hot cream mixture over corn
   - Add **3/4 of the cheddar cheese** (save rest for top)
   - Add chives/green onions if using
   - Add Parmesan if using
   - Stir gently but thoroughly

2. Pour into prepared baking dish
3. Spread evenly

### STEP 4: TOP (The Crown - 38.2% Enhancement)
1. Crush Ritz crackers:
   - In bowl, mix crushed crackers with 2 tablespoons melted butter
   - Add remaining 1/4 cheddar cheese to cracker mixture
   - Add bacon bits if using

2. Sprinkle evenly over corn mixture

### STEP 5: BAKE (The Transformation)
1. Bake at 350Â°F for **25-30 minutes** (Ï†-based timing)
   - Until bubbly around edges
   - Crackers golden brown
   - Cheese melted throughout

2. Let rest **5 minutes** before serving (allows TSR to stabilize)
