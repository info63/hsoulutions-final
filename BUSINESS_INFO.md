## What is D-U-N-S?

The D-U-N-S (Data Universal Numbering System) Number is a unique 9-digit identifier for businesses created by Dun & Bradstreet. It's used by:
- Federal and state government agencies
- Banks and lenders
- Suppliers and vendors
- Potential customers and partners
