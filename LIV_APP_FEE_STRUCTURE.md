**Challenge:** $10/month in USA ‚â† $10/month in developing countries

**Solution:** Purchase Power Parity (PPP) pricing

**Examples:**
- **USA:** $9.99/month

- **Brazil:** R$19.99/month (~$4 USD)

- Detect user's country via IP (or let them select)
- Adjust price to local purchasing power
- Same features, fair price everywhere
- **No VPN arbitrage** (verify payment method country matches)

**Why This Matters:**
- Shannon's vision: "Empower all to be the best at all things"
- Can't empower if price is barrier
- Knowledge should be accessible globally
- Still profitable (volume + fairness = sustainability)
