## Important Tax Notes

### Gifting Shares:

**2024 Gift Tax Rules:**
- $18,000 per person per year = no paperwork
- Above that = file Form 709 (gift tax return)
- But you have $13.61M lifetime exemption
- So no actual tax, just disclosure

**Example:**
- If 13,000 shares worth $1/share = $13,000 gift to Crystal
- Under $18,000 limit = no paperwork needed

**Example 2:**
- If 13,000 shares worth $10/share = $130,000 gift
- Over limit = file Form 709
- But applies to lifetime exemption, no tax due

### Selling Shares:

**Capital Gains:**
- If you sell shares for more than you paid (basis)
- You owe capital gains tax on profit
- Your basis = $0 or par value or what you contributed
- Long-term rate (held >1 year): 15-20%

**Example:**
- You sell 10,000 shares for $50,000
- Your basis: $100 (10,000 Ã— $0.01 par value)
- Gain: $49,900
- Tax: ~$7,485-9,980 (15-20% depending on income)
