### HYDRATION PROTOCOL

**Daily Target: 100+ oz water**

**Formula:**
- Bodyweight (lbs) √ó 0.67 = oz/day baseline
- + 20 oz per hour of practice/training
- + 16 oz for every lb lost during practice (weigh before/after)

**Timing:**
- 20 oz immediately upon waking
- 16 oz before practice
- 32+ oz during practice (sip constantly)
- 20 oz post-practice (with protein)
- 12+ oz with each meal

**Electrolytes:**
- Add to water during practice (sodium, potassium, magnesium)

- Critical in hot gyms, long practices, game days

**Signs You're Dehydrated:**
- Dark yellow urine (should be light/clear)
- Fatigue, headache, brain fog
- Poor performance (reaction time drops 10%+ when dehydrated)
