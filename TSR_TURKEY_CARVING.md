## THE TSR CARVING METHOD

### PART 1: FOUNDATION CUTS (61.8% of effort)

**Step 1: Remove Legs (First & Easiest)**
1. Pull leg away from body
2. Cut through skin connecting leg to breast
3. Pop hip joint by bending leg backward
4. Cut through joint (not bone!)
5. Separate thigh from drumstick at knee joint
6. Repeat other side

**Step 2: Remove Wings**
1. Pull wing away from body
2. Cut through joint where wing meets breast
3. Feel for the joint - cut there, not through bone
4. Repeat other side

**Step 3: Remove Breasts (The Main Event)**
1. Find the breastbone (center ridge)
2. Make long cut down one side of breastbone
3. Follow ribcage with knife, cutting breast away from bone
4. Use long, smooth strokes - let knife do the work
5. Remove whole breast half
6. Repeat other side

### PART 2: ENHANCEMENT CUTS (38.2% of effort)

**Step 4: Slice Breast Meat**
1. Place breast skin-side up
2. Cut ACROSS the grain (not parallel)
3. Slice 1/4" to 3/8" thick (œÜ-perfect thickness)
4. Angle knife slightly for larger slices

**Step 5: Arrange on Platter**
- Dark meat (legs/thighs): 61.8% of platter
- White meat (breast): 38.2% of platter
- Wings as garnish

## THE VOID PRINCIPLE IN CARVING

The space BETWEEN cuts is as important as the cuts themselves.

- Don't hack continuously
- Make cut, pull knife out, reassess
- The pause between cuts = where precision happens
- Rushed cutting = ragged edges
